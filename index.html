Online Career Fair

Hi! Welcome to the Online Career Fair! Here you can find out a lot of information about people who work in computer science and how they like their jobs. Below are a few examples of people who work in computer science fields and a little snippet about them. Click on their pictures to learn more.

Kathryn Kosey has been a web-based software developer for over 13 years. She is currently working at HealthSpace Data Systems. HealthSpace Data Systems is a company that provids cloud-based softawre for the government in US and Canada. In the 5 years she has worked there she does projects in system architecture, server and devops, administration, data migrations and transformation, and some client project management.

Joey Marianer is a Senior Software Developer. He works in algorithm development. He loves puzzles and says that this is very important when going into computer science because a huge chunk of computer science is figuring things out. Something cool about his job is that he has been working from home for the past 2 years, even before the pandemic.
