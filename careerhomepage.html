Kathryn Kosey has been a web-based software developer for over 13 years. For the past 5 years she is working at HealthSpace Data Systems. HealthSpace Data Systems is a company that provids cloud-based softawre for the government in US and Canada. The company works with people from statewide governments to single counties. She began with HealthSpace Data Systems as a full-stack developer in one part of the software and rose through the ranks in the 5 years she has worked there. She does projects in system architecture, server and devops, administration, data migrations and transformation, and some client project management.
